In the 'Ireland on Sunday' it has an article on what Liam Neeson will be up to next.

Irish actor Liam Neeson has decided to enter the sensitive arena of Northern politics again by narrating a documentary on the history of the Maze prison.
Neeson will narrate an RTE/Channel 4 co-production on the history of the troubled jail which permanently closed last month after 30 years.
It says the show will be shown on RTE 1 on Tuesday week.
