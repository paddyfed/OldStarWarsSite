Recently I have been thinking of a mascot or a character from the Star Wars universe that would represent our little group. I thought long and hard and I finally decided on Yoda. But I didn't want just ANY old Yoda, I wanted a unique Yoda. Well you cant get more unique than the Yoda from Rich Cando's 'Star Dudes'™. Rich has given me permission to use his Yoda as the mascot for our site which means you will be seeing more of this little guy in the future, and next time you are in Ireland, Rich, give us a call and I will buy you that Guinness :).
I have become concerned that there aren't as many people entering the trivia competition. I have decided to let this one run but if there aren't sufficent entries there may not be a second. I hope that all the members of the site will enter, after all it's only for fun.
