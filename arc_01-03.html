March 10, 2001

As I'm not sure where I'll be on St Patrick's Day this year I have decided to put up the 'decorations' a week early. So if I don't update next week (17th March) Happy St. Patrick's Day.

I would also like to warn those from the Republic who may be thinking of subscribing to the official Star Wars magazine that you need to pay in pounds Sterling not in Punts. I've learned this the hard way and I hope I've prevented someone from making the same mistake.

With the recent 'announcement' on TheForce.net that Episode II may be released worldwide at the same time that would make the countdown on the site wrong. However I have decided to keep it as it is until there is official word from Lucasfilm. The article can be found on TheForce.net's Episode II page.
