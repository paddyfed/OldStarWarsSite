November 21, 2001

The changeover to Version 3 continues. This time all the old editorials have been transferred. Also my home computer has been fixed and I'm in the process of transferring files to it.

November 17, 2001

Trailer C 'Forbidden Love' is now up on the StarWars.com site and I have to say that it gives me that tingly feeling which is good. I've also seen the other trailer, Trailer B 'Mystery', and it looks great too. I especially loved the last few lines.

November 13, 2001

Episode II release date confirmed, Thursday 16th May is the day everyone gets off work and camps out at the local cinema. I guess the 'One world, One release campaign' was heard by someone. Not only that but the teaser poster was officially announced as well. I think it would have looked better without the saber though.

On a completly different note, you may or may not know of the Blackout Ireland campaign that is taking place on Friday and I have decided to join in by taking down my two sites, this one and PaddyFedoo Online, offline for the day. For more information on the campaign go to blackout.irelandoffline.org. Also the news archives for October have been added.

November 8, 2001

Well I've seen the Episode II trailer and I think it looks brilliant! TV3 did show the trailer, as reported on TheForce.net but it was a day later than BBC and they didn't show it in full which really ruined it.
