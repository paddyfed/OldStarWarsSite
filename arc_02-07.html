July 13, 2002

On Sunday night at 1.30am, RTÉ 1 are showing George Lucas' first ever feature film: THX 1138. This is what the RTÉ Guide had to say about the film.

A sci-fi curio which has earned cult status chiefly for being the film that started the movie career of a young buck by the name of George Lucas. Adapted from Lucas's student film, THX 1138 stars Robert Duvall and Donald Pleasance and is set in a futuristic world where technology dictates and mankind is reduced to soulless drones. As a first film, it's an interesting effort. Wonder what ever happened to the tyro writer/director?
