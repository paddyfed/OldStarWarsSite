February 18, 2001

TheForce.net has updated their earlier report about the Sky Premier's showing of The Phantom Menace. It now appears that all four films will be shown on the channel on successive weeks, beginning on 17th March (St. Patrick's Day). At first I thought it was strange that they were starting with A New Hope but I see that they are trying to build up the excitement for the premiere of The Phantom Menace. Read the full article here

February 11, 2001

TheForce.net has reported that The Phantom Menace will be shown on Sky Premier sometime in the near future. This will be the first time that the film will be seen on British television and will be the first oppertunity for those Irish fans who have the channel to see it. I wouldn't expect to see the film on Irish terrestrial channels for at least another year. See the article here.

Liam Neeson was meantioned in the "Ireland on Sunday's" top 50 richest people in Ireland. While he wasn't in the top 50 he was one of the significant entertainers who were 'becoming rich' as the article stated. Other notable entrants in this list were Pierce Brosnan, Roy Keane and The Corrs.
