The Blackout

This website has been temporarily removed in support of the Blackout protest organized by IrelandOffline, an independent pressure group working to bring affordable flat-rate and broadband Internet access services to Ireland.

The withdrawal of this site is intended to highlight the growing disenchantment
of Irish consumers and businesses with the current lack of products and services that will allow them to access the Internet affordably.

This site will remain unavailable until midnight tonight, or in some cases for the rest of the weekend, that is until midnight on Sunday, the 18th of November. We apologise for any inconvenience caused, and hope that you understand the necessity.

For further information on the reasoning behind this protest, how how to get involved and support the protest yourself, please visit the IrelandOffline Blackout website.

Thank you,
The IrelandOffline Committee & Members
