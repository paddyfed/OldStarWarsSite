The film 'Excalibur' was on RTÉ 1 last night. Liam Neeson played one of the knights of the round table (Gawain) and he fought Sir Lancelot in a duel for Queen Guenevere's innocence. Another point of interest about this film is that someone once suggested that the plot of Episode II or Episode III would have similarities with this film.
