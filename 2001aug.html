August 26, 2001

Last thursday, 23 August, the Northern-Irish newspaper, The Irish News, caried an article about the Belfast Film Festival which started on Wednesday. Here is an excerpt:

Science fiction will be brought down to earth with a futuristic look at Belfast in the year 3001, as well as sci-fi offerings from cult directors George Lucas and Stanly Kubrick.
Pretty cool. As well as this they had a familiar character on the streets of Belfast launching the festival. For a scan of the full article click here.

August 8, 2001

Well, what a time to have our birthday celebrations. Big announcements have been made in the Star Wars world. The title for Episode II has been officially announced as 'Attack of the Clones'. Many people hate the title, saying it's too cheesy or sounds too much like 'clowns'. I, myself, like the title and don't have a problem with it. What do you think?

HAPPY BIRTHDAY TO THE ISW!!! On this day, exactly 1 year ago, I created this website for all Irish Star Wars fans. In that time we have had over 1400 visitors and 26 members joined the group.
